Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

I typically respond within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, please mention it in your message.

What information should I include for bug reports?

Please include your device model, iOS version, and detailed steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots or screen recordings are also very helpful!

Can I request a refund?

All refunds are processed through Apple's App Store. You can request a refund directly from Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Do you collect my personal data?

I only use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry. I don't share your data with third parties. Check each app's privacy policy for details.
